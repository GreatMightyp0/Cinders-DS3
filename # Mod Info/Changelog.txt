#------------------------------
# Changelog
#------------------------------

# Weapons
- Sidearms tweak:
 - Rosamarinus:
  - Now fire 3 bullets per L1 press.
  - Increases FP costs from 15 to 30.
  - Reduced effective range.
  - Turning during free aim mode should be more smooth now.
  - Should now hit target more consistently now.
  - Increases spread.
  - Increases Aux buildup for Sanguinus/Maledicinus/Venenus/Galcius.

 - Hunter's Blunderbuss:
  - Increases FP costs from 20 to 25.
  - Decreases the effectiveness of Fp/Hp steal effect for Magic and Dark infusion.
  - Reduced effective range.
  - Increases FP pause timer from 3 to 5 seconds.

 - Hunter's Pistol:
  - Reduced effective range. 

 - Repeating Pistol:
  - Reduced effective range.   
  - Increases Homming capability.

 - Evelynn
  - Reduced effective range.

- Slightly increases the radius of Chaos Blade Warcry Special attack.
- Slightly extended the lingering hitbox for Alternate Katana's 4th R1 when one handed.

# General
- Increased the stats of some character NPC so that they can meet the stat requirements of the equipment that they use.
- Changed the summary of Frozen Chaos Essence and Odessa's Crystal so that they mention their effects instead of reinforcement.

# Shields
- Reduced the stability of the Leather Shield from 75 to 62 (the same as the rest of small shields).
- Added air juggle stagger to the Dragonhead Greatshield WA.

# Bosses
- Mirror Knight:
 - Changed the fight so it is optional. It can be triggered by using the "Taunt" prompt.
 - Slowed the Mirror Knight animations down to 90% of DSA speed (was 110%).
 - Added two Mirror Phantom stages. 
  - The Mirror Knight will now fall down and his shield will emit a burst, spawning a Mirror Phantom. 
  - While the Mirror Knight is fallen, it is invulnerable to damage.
  - The first stage occurs at 75% HP, spawning the Mad Warrior.
  - The second stage occurs at 35% HP, spawning the Hallowed Knight.

# Memories
- Added the night skybox to the map.
- Added an announcer that notifies the player of each round.
- Changed the general structure of Memories:
 - Now comprised of five rounds: 1 and 2 are 'easy' mixes of enemies, 3 and 4 are 'tough' mixes of enemies and 5 is a miniboss.
 - Each round is two minutes long, with the miniboss round lasting until it is defeated.
 - After the miniboss is defeated, this process repeats until the player dies or leaves

- Added four new memories:
 - Memory of the Swamp
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Ghru (Spear)
   - Ghru (Curved Sword)
   - Ghru (Caster)
   - Ghru (Mad)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Spear)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Curved Sword)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Caster)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Mad)
   - Elder Ghru
   
 - Memory of the Wall
   - Contains the following enemy types:
    - Hollow Soldier (Sword and Shield)
    - Hollow Soldier (Crossbow)
    - Hollow Assassin
    - Large Hollow Soldier (Greataxe)
    - Large Hollow Soldier (Halberd)
    - Lothric Knight (Greatsword)
    - Lothric Knight (Spear)
    - Winged Knight (Twinaxes)
    
 - Memory of the Cathedral:
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Devout of the Deep
   - Large Devout of the Deep
   - Cathedral Evangelist
   - Writhing Infested Corpse
   - Cathedral Grave Warden
   - Cathedral Knight
   - Cathedral Guardian

 - Memory of the Settlement
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Peasant Hollow (Axe)

   - Hollow Slave (Pick)
   - Hollow Slave (Flamberge)
   - Hollow Manservant (Saw)
   - Hollow Manservant (Pot)
   - Curse-rotted Greatwood

- Fixed dummy polys for Burial Scythe.
- Holy/Enchanted Follower's Torch should now properly deal damage when Weapon Art is used.
