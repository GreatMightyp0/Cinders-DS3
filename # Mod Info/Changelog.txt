# Changelog
#------------------------------

# General
- Adjusted fall damage, removed the fall damage reduction from Dexterity and increased the one from Vitality. As a general rule, any non-lethal fall can now be survived if you have full HP with 70% equip load or less and 0 vitality.
- Added Holy Water Urn, sold by Irina for 400 Souls each. Also unlocked at the bonfire Item Forge after lighting the Cleansing Chapel bonfire.
- Added Throwing Frying Pan, sold by the Shrine Handmaid for 100 Souls each. Also unlocked at the bonfire Item Forge after lighting the Distant Manor bonfire.
- Undead Hunter Charm now also reduces all forms of healing received to 0.
- Duel Charm now also silences the target (prevents spell casting).

# Masteries
- Added mastery bonuses for achieving 99 in a stat (except Vigor). 
- You can enable the masteries at Ludleth.
    - Mastery of Attunement:
        - Increases passive FP restoration to 15 per second.
        
    - Mastery of Endurance:
        - Grants immunity to all non-lethal fall damage.
        
    - Mastery of Vitality:
        - Gain 100 attack deflection for 3 seconds every 9 seconds.
        
    - Mastery of Strength:
        - Boosts maximum equipment load by 25%.
        
    - Mastery of Dexterity:
        - Reduces weapon stamina consumption by 25%.
        
    - Mastery of Intelligence:
        - FP regeneration is no longer paused by any melee attack.
        
    - Mastery of Faith:
        - Doubles the effectiveness of HP restoration.
        
    - Mastery of Luck:
        - Gain a 5% chance to deal double damage with any attack.

- Note that using Reallocate Attributes will reset these flags, meaning you will need to return to Ludleth if you want to re-enable a Mastery (if the stat is still at 99).

# Journey Configuration 
- Added Explorer mode:
 - Effects:
    - Boosts absorptions by 30%.
    - Adds 1% HP regen per second.
    - Adds 30 stamina recovery.
    - Reduces soul gain by 25%.
    - Invalidates challenge runs.
    - Prevents affixing of accessories.
 - Intended as an 'easy mode' for those that want to explore the content in the mod.
- Wanderer Mode:
 - Adjusted the damage boosts from bosses, now varies between 0.25% and 1%, depending on the boss. Overall max is now 25%.

# Reinforcement
- Increased the base damage boost provided by reinforcement (now +30%, was +20%). Overall damage boost at +10 is now 25% compared to +0 version of weapon.

# Infusions
- Lethal infusion now removes any INT/FTH requirements the weapon might have.
- Lucky and Laughing infusions now equalize the stat requirements of the weapon/spell tool (Strength with Dexterity and Intelligence with Faith).
- Lucky infusion now changes all the scaling to be only Luck. As a general rule, the higher the stat requirements of a weapon, the more suitable it is for the Lucky infusion.

# Bosses
- Added the Marauder.
 - Appears in the Overgrown Sanctum as a neutral NPC.
 - Will only engage you once you have acquired 100,000 souls. Starting the boss fight will remove 100,000 souls.
 - Rewards 500,000 souls when killed.
 - Drops Soul of the Marauder, which can transposed into:
  - Fume Sword
  - Fume Ultra Greatsword
  - Knight Slayer's Ring
 
- Added Titanite Abomination.
 - Found in Archdragon Peak bridge area.
 - Drops Titanite Slab.
 
- Changed the Cathedral Guardian.
 - He is now found in the grounds below the small chapel.
 - The boss fight starts when you drop down or leave the lower part of the chapel.
 - His arena is all of the grounds, giving you a large arena to fight him in.
 - In certain spots he will warp to the player's area if he has been pathing trapped.
 - Increased HP to 8000 (was 5000).

# Weapons
- Adjusted the Bloodborne Dash animation when not locked-on.
- Adjusted the bullet radius of Rosamarinus (and the Infused variants). 
- Changed the vfx of Lucky Censuring Palm, Fool's Catalyst and Maledicinus.
- Changed the vfx of Blood, Dark and Fire Torches.
- Increase the length of Crescent Axe by 15%.
- Increased the inflict from the aux infusions of the Witchtree Limb to be on par with great hammers.
- Slightly adjusted Censuring Palm damage frame and event.
- Adjusted Fist Class stamina costs.
- Adjusted Fist/Halberd Class kick animation.
- Infused Follower Torches now have different coloured visual effects when using the Weapon Art, except for Blunt/Sharp/Fire/Lethal infusions.
- Flynn's Bow now scales only with Luck and requires 20 STR/DEX to be used.
- Changed the Weapon Art of the Obsidian Greatsword.
- Removed the +10 guard absorption Valorheart and Dark Hand gained when using elemental infusions.
- Adjusted the guard absorption and stability of several weapons.
- Added guard absorption and stability scaling to these weapons:
 - Ultra greatswords
 - Greataxes
 - Great hammers
 - Witchtree Limb
 - Dark Hand (only guard absorption)
 - Thorned Greatsword
 - Runelock
 - Valorheart
 - Giant Door Shield
 - Imperious Greatshield
- Guard absorption scales with Dexterity, stability scales with Strength. Both scale linearly and work indepently of damage scaling, infusion or reinforcement level.

# Shields

# Spells
- Black Flame Arc should visually appear on all weapons now.
- Changed the stats of Bursting Fireball and Black Fire Scatter and the way they work. They now only shoot 3 fireballs by default, and leveling intelligence increases the amount of fireballs shot per cast (capping at 31 intelligence).
- Changed Refined Black Fire Scatter to Great Black Fire Scatter, it now boosts damage and toxic inflict.
- Changed Black Fireball to use the Fireball cast animation.
