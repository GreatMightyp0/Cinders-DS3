#------------------------------
# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Weapons
- Sidearms tweak:
 - Rosamarinus:
  - Now fire 3 bullets per L1 press.
  - Increases FP costs from 15 to 30.
  - Reduced effective range.
  - Turning during free aim mode should be more smooth now.
  - Should now hit target more consistently now.
  - Increases spread.
  - Increases Aux buildup for Sanguinus/Maledicinus/Venenus/Galcius.

 - Hunter's Blunderbuss:
  - Increases FP costs from 20 to 25.
  - Decreases the effectiveness of Fp/Hp steal effect for Magic and Dark infusion.
  - Reduced effective range.
  - Increases FP pause timer from 3 to 5 seconds.

 - Hunter's Pistol:
  - Reduced effective range. 

 - Repeating Pistol:
  - Reduced effective range.   
  - Increases Homming capability.

 - Evelynn
  - Reduced effective range.

- Slightly increases the radius of Chaos Blade Warcry Special attack.
- Slightly extended the lingering hitbox for Alternate Katana's 4th R1 when one handed.

# Reinforcement
- Restored the reinforcement mechanic:
 - All weapons and shields are now reinforcable upto +10.
 - Reinforcement now only boosts base damage.
 - Reinforcement uses the standard Titanite set in all instances (2/4/6 Titanite Shard, 2/4/6 Large Titanite Shard, 2/4/6 Titanite Chunk, 1 Titanite Slab).
- Added Titanite across all maps, mostly present on corpses.

# Mythic Mode
- Renamed Tablet of Legends to Mythical Tablet

- Renamed Hard Mode to Mythic Mode.
- Now has the following effects:
 - Disables the ability to reinforce weapons and shields.
 - Unlocks special offerings from Magnum Ursus.
 - Reduces absorptions by 33%.
 - Reduces aux resistances by 33%.
 
# NPCs
- Magnum Ursus now offers his set to buy immediately.
- Magnum Ursus will now offer the following to buy based on boss kills:
 - Vordt unlocks Titanite Shards
 - Pontiff Sulyvahn unlocks Large Titanite Shards
 - Nameless King unlocks Titanite Chunks
 - Soul of Cinder unlocks Titanite Slabs
- Magnum Ursus now has a secret shop for those in Mythic mode.
- Moved Magnum Ursus to the other side of the sanctum.

# General
- Increased the stats of some character NPC so that they can meet the stat requirements of the equipment that they use.
- Changed the summary of Frozen Chaos Essence and Odessa's Crystal so that they mention their effects instead of reinforcement.
- Armors are now sorted by their passive effects. Armor sets with different effects on each piece or armor pieces with more than one effect will be at the bottom of the list.
- Added Tiny Mass of Humanity, changes the appearance of the player to a Humanity Sprite. Sold by Alchemist Jiji.
- Changed the coins description to make them mention they "Last until death or warp".

# Shields
- Reduced the stability of the Leather Shield from 75 to 62 (the same as the rest of small shields).
- Added air juggle stagger to the Dragonhead Greatshield WA.

# Spells
- Reduced the damage of the uncharged Old Moonlight projectile to 150 (charged is still 250).

# Bosses
- Mirror Knight:
 - Changed the fight so it is optional. It can be triggered by using the "Taunt" prompt.
 - Slowed the Mirror Knight animations down to 90% of DSA speed (was 110%).
 - Added two Mirror Phantom stages. 
  - The Mirror Knight will now fall down and his shield will emit a burst, spawning a Mirror Phantom. 
  - While the Mirror Knight is fallen, it is invulnerable to damage.
  - The first stage occurs at 75% HP, spawning the Mad Warrior.
  - The second stage occurs at 35% HP, spawning the Hallowed Knight.

# Memories
- Added the night skybox to the map.
- Added an announcer that notifies the player of each round.
- Memories now unlock as you visit different areas of the map.
- Changed the general structure of Memories:
 - Now comprised of five rounds: 
  - 1: simple mixture of enemies
  - 2: simple mixture of enemies
  - 3: hard mixture of enemies
  - 4: hard mixture of enemies
  - 5: boss
 - Each round is 3 minutes long, with the boss round lasting until it is defeated. Overall a memory will take about 15 minutes to complete.
 - The memory now ends once the boss is killed.
 - Completing a memory will award Titanite material and Primordial Fragment(s).

- Added new memories:
 - Memory of the Swamp
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Ghru (Spear)
   - Ghru (Curved Sword)
   - Ghru (Caster)
   - Ghru (Mad)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Spear)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Curved Sword)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Caster)
   - Smouldering Ghru (Mad)
   - Elder Ghru
   
 - Memory of the Wall
   - Contains the following enemy types:
    - Hollow Soldier (Sword and Shield)
    - Hollow Soldier (Crossbow)
    - Hollow Assassin
    - Large Hollow Soldier (Greataxe)
    - Large Hollow Soldier (Halberd)
    - Lothric Knight (Greatsword)
    - Lothric Knight (Spear)
    - Winged Knight (Twinaxes)
    
 - Memory of the Cathedral:
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Devout of the Deep
   - Large Devout of the Deep
   - Cathedral Evangelist
   - Writhing Infested Corpse
   - Cathedral Grave Warden
   - Cathedral Knight
   - Cathedral Guardian

 - Memory of the Settlement
  - Contains the following enemy types:
   - Peasant Hollow (Axe)
   - Peasant Hollow (Sickle)
   - Peasant Hollow (Mallet)
   - Hollow Slave (Pick)
   - Hollow Slave (Flamberge)
   - Hollow Manservant (Saw)
   - Hollow Manservant (Pot)
   - Aborr

# Map
- Added a Mantling mechanic. Certain locations will allow you to 'Hop' or 'Leap' over barriers or towards certain areas.

- Irithyll:
 - Added a new walkway just after the cave as you enter Irithyll. This allows you to fall down into the waterway as a shortcut.
 - Added a small rooftop area with a Fire Witch and a chest
 - Added a large rooftop area accessed via a small jumping puzzle. 
 - Moved Domhnall of Zena to the large rooftop area.

- Cemetery of Ash
 - Added a 'Journey Configuration' feature that forces you to pick from one of the available modes before allowing you to continue.
 - Moved the Dismal Knight into the Lonely Hollow area
 - Moved the starting spawn back to the original location.
 - Removed the Lonely Hollow bonfire.
 - Moved the Sanctum NPCs back into Firelink Shrine.
 
# Journey Configuration
- You can now choose to start in a different/random location. If the location is not the Cemetery of Ash, you will be teleported to it. This replaces the old note gifts.
- Skip Journey is now part of this menu, and advances you into your next NG+ cycle when used.
- You now need to select from one of the available game modes before you can start playing. This choice is irrevisible within the current journey.

 - Standard Mode:
    - No additional effects.
    
 - Wanderer Mode
    - Weaponry and shields can no longer be reinforced.
    - Bosses will grant a permanent damage boost after they have been defeated.
    
 - Mythic Mode
    - Weaponry and shields can no longer be reinforced.
    - World is populated by more enemies.
    - Reduces absorptions by 33%.
    - Reduces aux resistances by 33%.
    
 - Gauntlet Mode
    - Two types are available:
        - Set Order: you will face each boss in a predefined order.
        - Random Order: you will face a random boss each time. Once a boss is killed, it will no longer appear in the pool of available bosses.
    - Using the Firelink Shrine bonfire, you can use the "Next Boss"/"Face Boss" option to automatically teleport to the next boss in the gauntlet.
    - Map exploration is discouraged, if you do not engage the boss when teleported to them, your progress will be reset.
    - After each boss, you will be returned to Firelink Shrine.
    - The Fire Keeper will show you her Illustrious Collection, which allows you to purchase almost any item for souls.
    
# Bugfixes
- Fixed ammunition not displaying the full AR in the stat menu when equipped with a bow, greatbow or crossbow.
- Fixed dummy polys for Burial Scythe.
- Holy/Enchanted Follower's Torch should now properly deal damage when Weapon Art is used.
- Fixed Old Moonlight consuming hefty amount of FP and Stamina when charged.
- Rosamarinus and the elemental variant should now display the correct on hit effect.
- Fixed the description of some ascended weapon buff spells.
