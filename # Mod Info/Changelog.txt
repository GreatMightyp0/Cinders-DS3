# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Journey Configuration
- Mythic Mode:
 - Curses no longer grant soul absorption bonuses.

# Covenant
- Added a Devotion system for each covenant:
    - While you have the covenant's accessory equipped, you can increase your devotion in exchange for the covenant material.
    - Increasing devotion will increase the rank of the accessory, improving its effect.
    
- Added Priestess Emilia, a covenant NPC for Way of Blue. Appears after you receive the accessory from High Priestess Emma or the Dancer is killed.
- Added Knight Lun, a covenant NPC for Blue Sentinels. Appears after you receive the accessory from Horace in Halfway Fortress or Aldrich is killed.
- Changed the covenant material/consumable drop rate: instead of being a low but unrestricted rate, the chance is now much higher (25%) but there is a internal cooldown of 20 seconds before another drop can occur. For consumables this cooldown is 40 seconds.
 - This is disincentivise camping large hordes of enemies and instead rewards consistent kills over time.

# Infusions
- Fire infusion now increases max Stamina by 20%.
- Lightning infusion now boosts Armor Poise by 20%.

# Ammunitions
- Adjusted the behavior of Splintering Bolts. Should now behave like other bleed when proc'ed.

# Weapons
- Added guard and stability scaling to the Farron Greatsword, Ringed Knight Paired Greatswords and Missionary Greataxe.

- Switch left/right model of the Beast Claw.
- Smooth several transitions between attacks for the new moveset.
- Adjusted the stamina costs of Ringed Knight Paired Greatsword.
- Increased the radius of Morne's Rage, Holy Wrath & Wrath of the Gods (Wolnir's Holy Sword) to be on par with the spell version.
- Bloodborne Dash/Beast Lunge now no longer have Instability Frames, stamina cost is increased from 20>25 as the result.
- Greatsword of Judgment Weapon Art now fires Pontiff Sulyvahn's projectile, Light Attack for Soul Spear, Heavy Attack for Purple Wave.
- Lances now have their own Weapon Class:

# Consumables
- Adjusted several oil visual.
- Added the following oils:
 - Psychedelic Oil
 - Brume Oil
- All damage buffing resin consumables now add 100 damage.
- Removed Kukri, Spellbound Kukri, Charcoal Pine Bundle and Gold Pine Bundle.
- Added new spellbound item versions of all throwing knives and the other kind of firebombs/urns.
- Added new spellbound item versions of each resin, they add 100 damage + 10% and last 10 seconds, aux resins have higher build up and last 20 seconds, and both types use the bundle animation.
- Added Spellbound Pellet, grants immunity to damage for 5 seconds.
- Added Spellbound Mossfruit, grants immunity to status effects for 10 seconds.
- Changed how the spellbound items work:
 - Removed their Luck scaling and made them better by default.
 - They require to be charged to be used.
 - Charges are gained by attacking enemies with any melee weapon, the amount gained usually depends on the weapon class.
 - When there are not enough charges the item's icon darkens and can't be used.
 - The amount of charges required varies per item.
 - Charges can be "stored" to use the items more than once consecutively, but there is a max limit.

- Added the following illusion/insignia:

 - Elite Lothric Knight Helm (Opened Helm)
