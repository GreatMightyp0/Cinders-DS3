# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Weapons
- Changed moveset for the following weapon class:
 - Claw 		
  - Claws (Stance)
  - Manikin Claws (Leaping Slash)
  - Beast Claws (Bestial Pounce)

 - Ultra Greatsword 	
  - Fume Ultra Greatsword (Raime's Fury)
  - Fume Slated Swords (Nadalia's Wrath), a paired weapon (Fume Ultra Greatsword with Fume Sword), Fume Sword (Straight Sword) is removed.

- Switch left/right model of the Beast Claw.
- Smooth several transitions between attacks for the new moveset.
- Bloodborne Dash/Beast Lunge now no longer have Instability Frames, stamina cost is increased from 20>25 as the result.
- Greatsword of Judgment Weapon Art now fires Pontiff Sulyvahn's projectile, Light Attack for Soul Spear, Heavy Attack for Purple Wave.
- Lances now have their own Weapon Class:
 - Irithyll Lance
 - Greatlance
 - Rampart Golem Lance

- Minor Moveset Changes:
 - Rolling 1H R1 for Alternate Scythe moveset.
 - 4th 1H R1 for Alternate Straight Sword moveset. 

# Bugfixes
- Fixed the stat requirements of the uninfused and lucky infused Great Club.
- Fixed the visual effect on hit of Surging Soulmass.
- Fixed several weapon hitbox:
 - Giant's Halberd
 - Bone Scythe
 - Scythe of Nahr Alma
- Fixed new oils vfx not showing up for dual weapons.
