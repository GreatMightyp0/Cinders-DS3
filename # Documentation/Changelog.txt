#------------------------------
# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Gauntlet Mode
- Added a new NPC: Oathkeeper Xavius to host the Gauntlet menu.
 - The Fire Keeper is now absent in Gauntlet mode.
 - He is found in sitting on a chair in the corner that the Fire Keeper occasionally stands in.
- Removed access to most of the other shops in Firelink Shrine so that Oathkeeper Xavius is the main source of items.
 - Jiji and Grudore shops remain since they don't overlap with Xavius.
- Restored access to the memories while in Gauntlet Mode.
- Begin new journey is now accessible after you kill the Soul of Cinder, you no longer need to kill every boss to unlock it while in Gauntlet Mode.
- The starting items for Gauntlet Mode are now found near Oathkeeper Xavius as treasures.

# Weapons
- Changed default Farron Greatsword WA back to Parry.
 - Added method to get Stomp version of Farron Greatsword.

# Rings
- Ring of Momentum: 
 - Now adds 15/13/10/8/6/3 stamina cost to quickstep roll. 
 - Affixable.

- Bell of Grace
 - Now extends jump distance by 75/100/125/150/175/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Dashmaster's String
 - Now increases movement speed by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Ring of Retreat
 - Now increases backstep distance by 15/30/50/65/80/100%.
 - Affixable.
 
# Map
- Memory of Endurance:
 - Closed off the area behind the fallen tower and added collision.

- Smouldering Lake:
 - Overhauled the lake area with new objects.
 - Moved Helia of the Lake to a new location.
 - Removed 2 of the sandworms.
 - Added the 2 removed sandworm treasure to 2 new corpse treasures.
 - Added 3 patrolling Demons
 - Added 1 patrolling Demon Cleric

- Cemetery of Ash:
 - Sword Master now patrols the flat roof area that leads to the rafters.

# NPC
- Added Abyss Watcher Vernix:
 - Found in the Abyss Watcher boss room after they have been defeated.
 - Allows you to transfuse the Abyss Watcher soul into Farron Greatsword (Parry) or Farron Greatsword (Stomp).

# Phantoms
- Phantom prompts now appear as summon signs instead of phantom form.
- Added Sword Master as a summonable phantom in Untended Graves for Champion Gundyr. Replaces old-type summon.
- Sellsword Luet: adjusted AI.
- Maldron: adjusted AI. Will now point down on weak enemies randomly.

# AI
- Sword Master: updated AI.
- Butcher: updated AI.
- Greirat: updated AI.
- Kirk: updated AI. Will taunt you if you get low.
- Alva: updated AI.
 
# Bugfixes
- Fixed missing warp list title in warp menu.
- Fixed several Demon Clerics in m38 that lacked fire orb support
- Darksign is now given after Journey Configuration, to prevent warping out before starting a journey. 
- Fixed Corrupted Gundyr BGM playing incorrectly.
- Fixed Way of Blue description.
