# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Gauntlet Mode
- Added a new NPC: Oathkeeper Xavius to host the Gauntlet menu.
 - The Fire Keeper is now absent in Gauntlet mode.
 - He is found in sitting on a chair in the corner that the Fire Keeper occasionally stands in.
- Removed access to most of the other shops in Firelink Shrine so that Oathkeeper Xavius is the main source of items.
 - Jiji and Grudore shops remain since they don't overlap with Xavius.
- Restored access to the memories while in Gauntlet Mode.
- Begin new journey is now accessible after you kill the Soul of Cinder, you no longer need to kill every boss to unlock it while in Gauntlet Mode.
- The starting items for Gauntlet Mode are now found near Oathkeeper Xavius as treasures.
- Added Ring of Sacrifice to gauntlet shop.
- Added the covenant items and each rank of it to a Covenant shop at Oathkeeper Xavius.

# Classes
- Renamed Summoner class to Conjurator
 - Changed loadout to use the new Pyromancy based conjuration spells.

# Weapons
- Changed default Farron Greatsword WA back to Parry.
 - Added method to get Stomp version of Farron Greatsword.
- Reduced minimum stamina for Cannon/Church Cannon to 80.
- Reduced FP pause from WA to 2 seconds.
- Tackle WA is now much faster.

# Spell Tools
- Adjusted several of the dark catalysts so they now cast specific combinations of Sorcery/Miracle/Pyromancy instead of them all having the ability to cast all three.
- Adjusted the INT/FTH distribution for several catalysts.
- Added affinity to a specific damage type to each catalyst. Note that having 2 catalysts out at the same time will nullify this effect.

# Armor
- Gauntlets/Leggings now share the same icon as the Torso. This has been done mainly to free up icon space.
- Added a border flair to the armor icons so they are visually easier to scan through.

# Rings
- Ring of Momentum: 
 - Now adds 15/13/10/8/6/3 stamina cost to quickstep roll. 
 - Affixable.

- Bell of Grace
 - Now extends jump distance by 75/100/125/150/175/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Dashmaster's String
 - Now increases movement speed by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Ring of Retreat
 - Now increases backstep distance by 15/30/50/65/80/100%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Gower's Ring of Retribution (was Gower's Ring of Protection)
 - Grants 10/12/14/16/18/20% chance to cause Shatter for 5 seconds whenever you are hit on the back. Shatter causes your attack to deal 100% more damage.
 - Affixable.
 
- Old Man's Gambit
 - Grants 5.0% chance to cause Shatter. Shatter causes your attack to deal 100% more damage.
 - Affixable.
 
- Hornet Ring
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes by 50/60/70/80/90/100%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Assassin's Mark
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes from daggers by 100/120/140/160/180/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Elden Ring
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes from halberds by 100/120/140/160/180/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Carthus Milkring:
 - Reduces roll stamina consumption by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Lengthens your roll distance by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Greatwood Remnants:
 - Increases physical defense by 50/60/70/80/90/100% but shortens your roll distance by 40%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Fool's Sigil:
 - Cycles between beneficial effects every 10 seconds, but movement is randomised too.
  - Strength: boosts attack by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Defence: boosts absorption by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Fervor: boosts stamina recovery by 10/20/30/40/50/60.
  - Focus: reduces stamina and FP consumpion by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Movement speed is also adjusted, between -50% and +50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Ring of Conjuration:
 - Boosts damage of conjured allies by 20/40/60/80/100/150%.
 - Affixable.
 
# Covenants
- Renamed Ritualist Pact convenat item to Conjurator's Pact.
 - Changed effect to boost absorption of conjured allies by 10/20/30/40/50%.
 
# Items
- Undead Hunter Charm now reduces HP recovery effectiveness to 0%, preventing other forms of healing too.
- Draconic Fire/Draconic Might/Draconic Fire Orbs and Draconic Fury no longer require you to be naked to use.
 
# Spells
- Restored FP pause after cast: 
 - 3 seconds for most spells.
 - 1 second for dart/flashsword spells.
 
- Changed the FFX of Path of Frost to a nicer one.

- Added Teleport spell:
 - Teleport in the direction of travel. Lack of movement will result in no change of position.
 - Occurs as soon as you cast, allowing you to cover a large distance quickly. However it will cost FP each time, making it expensive to do so.
 - Transposed from Aldrich soul.

# Conjuration
- Added conjuration spells to Pyromancy, that act like the old companion system.
 - Conjure Skeleton
  - Found in Undead Settlement.
 - Conjure Hollow
  - Sold by Conjurator Ellie.
 - Conjure Hound
  - Sold by Conjurator Ellie.
 - Conjure Warrior (Carthus Swordsman)
  - Found in Smouldering Lake.
 - Conjure Sentinel (Lothric Knight)
  - Found in Lothric Castle.
 - Conjure Lycanthrope
  - Found in Road of Sacrifice.
 - Conjure Knight (Black Knight)
  - Transposed from Marauder soul.
 - Conjure Watcher (Abyss Watcher)
  - Transposed from Abyss Watcher soul.
 - Conjure Gargoyle
  - Found in Profaned Capital.
- These can be used in any map, and multiple can be active at once.
- They last 50 seconds, after which you must re-use the spell to conjure them again.
- They are immune to status effects.

- Added Ferverous Ritual:
 - Boosts the damage of conjured allies by 20% and increases stamina recovery by 30. 
 - Lasts 30 seconds.
 
- Added Major Restoration Ritual:
 - Restores 50% HP of conjured allies over 1 second.

- Added Minor Restoration Ritual:
 - Restores 20% HP of conjured allies over 1 second.
 
- Added Sacrificial Ritual:
 - Sacrifice self, reducing max HP by 99%. 
 - Empower active conjured allies massively, boosting damage by 100% and granting immunity to damage.
 - Lasts 30 seconds.
