# Changelog
#------------------------------
# Gauntlet Mode
- Added a new NPC: Oathkeeper Xavius to host the Gauntlet menu.
 - The Fire Keeper is now absent in Gauntlet mode.
 - He is found in sitting on a chair in the corner that the Fire Keeper occasionally stands in.
- Removed access to most of the other shops in Firelink Shrine so that Oathkeeper Xavius is the main source of items.
 - Jiji and Grudore shops remain since they don't overlap with Xavius.
- Restored access to the memories while in Gauntlet Mode.
- Begin new journey is now accessible after you kill the Soul of Cinder, you no longer need to kill every boss to unlock it while in Gauntlet Mode.
- The starting items for Gauntlet Mode are now found near Oathkeeper Xavius as treasures.

# Weapons
- Changed default Farron Greatsword WA back to Parry.
 - Added method to get Stomp version of Farron Greatsword.
- Reduced minimum stamina for Cannon/Church Cannon to 80.
- Reduced FP pause from WA to 2 seconds.

# Armor
- Gauntlets/Leggings now share the same icon as the Torso. This has been done mainly to free up icon space.
- Added a border flair to the armor icons so they are visually easier to scan through.

# Rings
- Ring of Momentum: 
 - Now adds 15/13/10/8/6/3 stamina cost to quickstep roll. 
 - Affixable.

- Bell of Grace
 - Now extends jump distance by 75/100/125/150/175/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Dashmaster's String
 - Now increases movement speed by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Ring of Retreat
 - Now increases backstep distance by 15/30/50/65/80/100%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Gower's Ring of Retribution (was Gower's Ring of Protection)
 - Grants 10/12/14/16/18/20% chance to cause Shatter for 5 seconds whenever you are hit on the back. Shatter causes your attack to deal 100% more damage.
 - Affixable.
 
- Old Man's Gambit
 - Grants 5.0% chance to cause Shatter. Shatter causes your attack to deal 100% more damage.
 - Affixable.
 
- Hornet Ring
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes by 50/60/70/80/90/100%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Assassin's Mark
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes from daggers by 100/120/140/160/180/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Elden Ring
 - Increases damage of backstabs and ripostes from halberds by 100/120/140/160/180/200%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Carthus Milkring:
 - Reduces roll stamina consumption by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Lengthens your roll distance by 25/30/35/40/45/50%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Greatwood Remnants:
 - Increases physical defense by 50/60/70/80/90/100% but shortens your roll distance by 40%.
 - Affixable.
 
- Fool's Sigil:
 - Cycles between beneficial effects every 10 seconds, but movement is randomised too.
  - Strength: boosts attack by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Defence: boosts absorption by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Fervor: boosts stamina recovery by 10/20/30/40/50/60.
  - Focus: reduces stamina and FP consumpion by 15/20/25/30/35/40%.
  - Movement speed is also adjusted, between -50% and +50%.
 - Affixable.
