# General
- Changed several damage boosting effects: If an effect increases the damage of both weapons and spells, now it also boosts the damage of throwable items (except Flynn's Ring).
- Adjusted the behavior of Splintering Bolts. Should now behave like other bleed when proc'ed.
- Added details to several menu descriptions.

- Fixed the visual effect on hit of Surging Soulmass.
- Fixed several weapon hitbox:
 - Giant's Halberd
 - Bone Scythe
 - Scythe of Nahr Alma
- Fixed new oils vfx not showing up for dual weapons.
- Fixed Katana unsheath animation.
- Heavy followup of the Ringed Knight Paired Greatsword Weapon Art now correctly deal damage on the slam and the final burst.
- Fixed the scabbard position during the 2H second R2 of Alternate Katana moveset.
- Fixed Stone of Draconic Fire/Stone of Draconic Might.
- Fixed Dragon Bone Fist WA.
- Fixed Moonlight Sword having 999 durability.
- Greirat will no longer show the pillage option (as it has been removed).
- Fixed Scribe Vadan's dialogue.
- You can now enter the Cathedral Guardian fight via the pit.
- Fixed the messages in Dreg Heap showing the incorrect text.
- Fixed Halflight's Intelligence and Faith stats.
- Fixed Blooming Purple Moss Clump not healing poison.
- Fixed Divine Relief not increasing equip load by 40%.
- Marauder talk interaction should no longer break if you start his boss fight and then leave without killing him.
- Fixed Makoto losing its aux effects when dark infused.
- Added the missing Dark Gem in Karla's shop.
- Fixed Lingering Acid Surge not applying the debuff.
- Added several missing weapons and Millwood Greatarrows to the Gauntlet mode shop.
- Fixed the storm type spells using more FP than they should.
- Fixed the Earthern Peak bonfire not unlocking all its forge items.
- Fixed the visual effects of Lingering Poison/Toxic Mist and Lingering Acid Surge.
- Old man's Gambit and Luck Mastery now also double the elemental damage portion of weapons that have it.
- Fixed the damage and duration from the toxic effect of the dung items.
 
# Journey Configuration
- Mythic Mode:
 - Curses no longer grant soul absorption bonuses.

# Covenant
- Added a Devotion system for each covenant:
    - While you have the covenant's accessory equipped, you can increase your devotion in exchange for the covenant material.
    - Increasing devotion will increase the rank of the accessory, improving its effect.
    
- Added Priestess Emilia, a covenant NPC for Way of Blue. Appears after you receive the accessory from High Priestess Emma or the Dancer is killed.
- Added Knight Lun, a covenant NPC for Blue Sentinels. Appears after you receive the accessory from Horace in Halfway Fortress or Aldrich is killed.

- Changed the covenant material/consumable drop rate: instead of being a low but unrestricted rate, the chance is now much higher (25%) but there is a internal cooldown of 20 seconds before another drop can occur. For consumables this cooldown is 40 seconds.
 - This is disincentivise camping large hordes of enemies and instead rewards consistent kills over time.

- Added covenant shop for Blue Sentinels.
- Added covenant shop for Way of Blue.
- Added covenant shop for Thieves' Pact

# Consumable Items
- Increased the absorption buff the bug pellets give to 20% for both PVE and PVP.
- All damage buffing resin consumables now add 100 damage.
- Removed Kukri, Spellbound Kukri, Charcoal Pine Bundle and Gold Pine Bundle.
- Added new spellbound item versions of all existing throwing knives and the other kind of firebombs/urns.
- Added new spellbound item versions of all existing resins, they add 100 damage + 10% and last 10 seconds, aux resins have higher build up and last 20 seconds, and both types use the bundle animation.
- Added Blessed Pine Resin, adds physical damage by 100 and boosts damage against undead enemies by 5% for 60 seconds. Sold by Irina or unlocked at the item forge after lighting the Scribe's Retreat bonfire.
- Added Spellbound Blessed Resin, adds physical damage by 100, further increases it by 10% and boosts damage against undead enemies by 5% for 10 seconds. Unlocked with the Giant's Coal.
- Added Cursed Pine Resin, adds 15 Curse build up for 60 seconds. Unlocked at item forge by lighting the Irithyll Dungeon bonfire.
- Added Spellbound Cursed Resin, adds 20 Curse build up for 20 seconds. Unlocked with the Profaned Coal.
- Added Spellbound Pellet, grants immunity to damage for 5 seconds.
- Added Spellbound Mossfruit, grants immunity to status effects for 10 seconds.
- Church Guardian Shiv is now Spellbound Guardian Shiv. Removed the scaling and increased its damage to 300.
- Ritual Spear Fragment is now a consumable item, with a max carry of 10. Reduced its price to 2,500 Souls and 1 Filianore's Spear Ornament per unit.
- Divine Spear Fragment is now Spellbound Spear Fragment. Removed the scaling and increased its damage to 1050 [875]. Increased its price to 50,000 Souls and 15 Filianore's Spear Ornaments.
- Young Grass Dew is now Spellbound Grass Dew. Increased the Stamina recovery to +10 and the absorptions to +10%.
- Elemental throwing knifes have unique looks when in the air and when impacting something.

- Changed how the spellbound items work:
 - Removed their Luck scaling and made them better by default.
 - They require to be charged to be used.
 - Charges are gained by attacking enemies with any melee weapon, the amount gained usually depends on the weapon class.
 - When there are not enough charges the item's icon darkens and can't be used.
 - The amount of charges required varies per item.
 - Charges can be "stored" to use the items more than once consecutively, but there is a max limit.

- Changed the draconic stones, they are now:
 - Spellbound Draconic Fire: Emits dragon fire breath, dealing 600 fire damage with no scaling. Gives spellbound charges.
 - Spellbound Draconic Might: Emits dragon might, dealing 2000 fire damage with no scaling and buffs the player with +10% damage boost and infinite poise for 30 seconds.
 - Spellbound Draconic Fire Orbs: Emits dragon fire orbs, dealing 500 fire damage with no scaling. Gives spellbound charges, but less than Spellbound Draconic Fire.
 - Spellbound Draconic Fury: Emits daconic might + buff, and summons ghost dragonoid that breathes fire dealing 1000 [250] fire damage.
